About Us

About Us!

At Genevieve’s Garden, we pride ourselves on being more than just a meal provider. We warmly welcome everyone in our community, especially those facing hardships like homelessness or low income. Our mission extends beyond nourishing meals; we're committed to helping individuals in any way we can. If you need assistance or support, don't hesitate to ask – we're here to help in every way possible.

Our Founder

Originally founded by Brother Henry, Genevieve's Garden has been a community staple since 2020, and we are excited to continue serving for many more years.

Our Leader

At the helm is JL, our daring leader, who is always ready to lend a hand and answer any questions you might have if you happen to meet him.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • "What do we need to recieve services?"

    To access our services, simply provide your name – that's all we require. There's no need for an address, age, or any form of documentation. Our approach is minimalist, and we ask for your name solely to address you personally.

  • "When do you open?"

    Our operating hours are Monday through Friday, from 12 PM to 1 PM, and we consistently ensure our availability during these times.

  • "Is there a menu for the meals?"

    Currently, our meal offerings are determined by the donations we receive, with distribution guided by factors such as shelf life and priority for consumption. Despite this, we do ensure that our selections include vegetarian options. Courtesy of Hollywood FoodCo.

  • "Do you give clothes everyday?"

    We don't provide clothing daily. Instead, we collaborate with our donation partner, Project Ropa , to offer clothing distributions once a week, typically on Fridays. This is aligned with our popular Taco Friday event, where we serve tacos to our guests.

  • "I am in need of other resources such as housing, can you help me?"

    Although we don't offer housing services directly, there are other programs in Hollywood that can assist, such as the Salvation Army or the Center, available Monday through Friday.

  • "If I have other questions, who can I ask?"

    Feel free to approach any member of our team with your questions. If we don't have the answers immediately, we'll make inquiries on your behalf. Rest assured, we're committed to providing you with the most satisfactory responses to meet your needs.
